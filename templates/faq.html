Below are frequently asked questions, you may find the answer for yourself

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) page, designed to address your queries and provide comprehensive information about our Roren Auto Spares Website. Explore this page to find detailed answers to common inquiries, covering topics such as product compatibility, warranties, returns, and more. If you can't find the information you need, our dedicated customer support team is just a message away, ready to assist you in making informed decisions for a seamless and satisfying shopping experience.

How can I find the right auto parts for my vehicle?
Utilize our user-friendly search feature by entering your vehicle's make, model, and year. Our extensive catalog ensures you get accurate results, making it easy to find the perfect fit for your car.
Are your auto parts genuine or aftermarket?
Our inventory includes both genuine OEM parts and high-quality aftermarket alternatives. Each product's description provides clarity on its origin, empowering you to make an informed decision based on your preferences and budget.
How do I track my order after making a purchase?
Once your order is processed and shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number. Use this number on our website or the carrier's platform to monitor the real-time status and expected delivery date of your order.
What payment methods do you accept for auto parts purchases?
We accept secure payments through various methods, including major credit cards, PayPal, and other reputable online payment options. Rest assured, your financial information is encrypted to ensure a safe and smooth transaction.
Do you provide installation services for the purchased auto parts?
Currently, we don't offer installation services. We recommend consulting a certified mechanic for precise and safe installation, ensuring your purchased auto parts function optimally and maintain the integrity of your vehicle.
How can I get assistance if I have a technical question about a specific auto part?
Our knowledgeable customer support team is just a call or email away. Reach out for expert guidance on any technical inquiries, and we'll provide detailed information to help you make informed decisions about your auto parts purchase.
What steps should I take if my auto part arrives damaged or defective?
We apologize for any inconvenience. If your auto part arrives damaged or defective, please contact our customer support immediately. Provide photos of the damage, and we'll promptly initiate a replacement or refund, ensuring your satisfaction with our products and service. Refer to our Returns page for more information on damaged or defective items.
Can I get help with finding compatible accessories or add-ons for the auto part I'm purchasing?
Absolutely! Our customer support team is ready to assist you in identifying suitable accessories or complementary components for your chosen auto part. Whether it's performance enhancements, installation kits, or maintenance tools, we can provide recommendations to enhance your driving experience.
